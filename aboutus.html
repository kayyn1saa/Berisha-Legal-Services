About Us


Berisha Business & Legal Consulting is a specialized consultancy dedicated to high-quality legal drafting, EU acquis harmonisation, and evidence-based policy development. Our team of experts brings diverse experience across public finances, energy and energy efficiency, civil law, civil service reform, and sector-specific regulatory frameworks. We support public institutions, businesses, and NGOs with clear, practical, and compliant legal solutions, helping them align with national and EU standards while strengthening governance, transparency, and operational effectiveness.
